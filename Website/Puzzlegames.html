Our Puzzle Games

Spot the Difference

The spot the difference puzzle game consists of two images that seem similar yet are deceptively different. The two images will contain slight differences between eachother which as the player you have to pinpoint where the differences between the two images are. The aim of the puzzle is to find every difference between the two images.

Sliding Blocks Puzzle

A sliding blocks puzzle consists of an image that is sperated into several pieces that are placed within a grid as block pieces. Within the grid one block space, where you would expect a part of the image to go is left blank. This is so that when the user clicks one of the blocks surrounidng this empty position then that block will move into that position and its original space will become blank. The aim of the puzzle is to re-construct the original image.

Snakes and Ladders

Snakes and ladders is a tradtional two or more player board game where the players take it in turns to roll a dice and then move their corresponidng piece by that many places. The winner is the first player to reach the end of the game board. However, this is harder than it seems because if a player lands on the head of a snake they have to move thir piece to the end of the snake. In contrast if a player's piece lands at the bottom of a ladder then they have to move their piece to the top of the ladder.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe otherwise known as Noughts and Crosses is a two player game whereby each player chooses between being the player using crosses(X) or Noughts(O) and then take it in turns to place one of their pieces on a 3x3 grid. It is a quick and simple game that can be played between two people quite easily jsut using pen and paper. The winner of the game is the person who is able to create a row, column or diagonal connection between three of their corresponding pieces.

Match The Pairs

Match the pairs is a game where there are a nubmer of cards placed face-down, each card must have another card that is exactly the same also placed down. It can be played as s singleplayer game or two player game. The player/players have to flip two cards at a time to check if the cards are the same, if they are the same then they keep the pair, if they are not the same then they turn them both back over. A player can have another turn if they successfully match a pair. The game is over when all pairs have successfully been matched and the winner if there are two or more players is the person with the most pairs. It is a great game for training ones memory as the best players are those that can remember the positions of previously turned over cards.
