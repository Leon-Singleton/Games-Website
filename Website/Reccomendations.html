Worlds Hardest Game

It claims to be the world's hardest game and it does not dissapoint, it very well could be the worlds hardest game. The game begins reasonably simple, as the player you have to move your red square from the first green safe zone to the last green safe zone. However yo must do so by avoiding thee moving obstacles in your path. The game becomes progressivley harder as the levels are completed to the point where it seems impossible to complete. The game requires lots of concentration and logical thinking so as to predict the movements of obstacles during the game so that they can be avoided. The game keeps a track of the deaths that you have had during your play of the game, and this is a great way to be able to compare with friends whos the better player, along with who gets to the highest level of course. Bee Brilliant

Bee brilliant is a game that is a great for kids, it is all about exploring the world of crazy bees and cheeky spiders. The aim is to connect the colourful lines of bees, in order to release the bees that are frozen. The music is extremely catchy and can be funny at times especially after each time you complete a level. There are 155 levels to complete so the game becomes very addictive and progressively more challenging, the more you play the more addicted you become. I feel it has beneifts to anyone that plays it as it requires the use of ones logical reasoning in order to connect as many bees as possible in a single instance so that you can generate a high enough score to unlock the following level. The game is available to play from both the android and IOS appstores and links are provided below:
